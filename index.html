INDIAN HIP-HOP

Indian hip hop is a part of the South Asian hip hop culture, is a genre of popular music developed in India. Desi hip hop is a term for music and culture which combines the influences of hip hop and the Indian subcontinent; the term desi referring to the South Asian diaspora.

EMIWAY BANTAI

Bilal Sheikh popularly known as Emiway Bantai(born 13 November 1995), is one of the biggest independent rap artists of India.Emiway Bantai is a very popular Indian Rapper, Singer, Composer, and Song Writer known for his amazing rap and Songs. He also runs a YouTube channel where he uploads Rap songs. He runs a popular record label named as 'BANTAI RECORDS'.

RAFTAAR

Dilin Nair (born 16 November 1988), better known by his stage name Raftaar, is an Indian rapper, lyricist, dancer, TV personality and music composer associated with Hindi and Punjabi music.He started working with Yo Yo Honey Singh as a part of the group Mafia Mundeer. He runs a popular record label named as 'KALAMKAR'

DIVINE

Vivian Wilson Fernandes (born 2 October 1990), better known by his stage name DIVINE, is an Indian rapper and songwriter from Mumbai, Maharashtra.He started to gain popularity after the release of his song "Yeh Mera Bombay" (2013). He broke through with the release of his song "Mere Gully Mein" featuring fellow Mumbai rapper Naezy. He runs record label named as 'GULLY GANG'

KR$NA

Krishna Kaul known as KR$NA) and formerly as Young Prozpekt, is a rapper from New Delhi, India. He is one of the earliest rappers to emerge in the Indian hip hop scene in the mid-2000s under the stage name Prozpekt. He is signed with KALAMKAR label

MC STAN

Altaf Shaikh also known as MC Stan is an Indian hip-hop rapper. He is from Pune, Maharashtra, India. He was born on 30 August 1999 and he is 22 Years Old years old. MC Stan got famous from his song “Wata”.He is also one of the biggest independent artist in India.MC Stan is highly inspired by Tupac Shakur ( an American Rapper). He follows the Tupac’s style of rapping.
